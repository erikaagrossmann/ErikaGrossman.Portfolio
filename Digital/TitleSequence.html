Title Sequence

Created Fall 2025

For this project we were instructed to make a title sequence for a show, video game, music video, etc. I chose the iconic show Sex and the City. To make this project I first picked a song that I felt fit (used is Oliva Dean's So Easy (To Fall in Love)), and I modified it slightly in Premiere Pro to make it fit in a minute duration with a nice ending. I then gathered personal assets (all photos used are original shot by me), and edited them in Photoshop, mostly to crop out the background. Lastly I imported everything into AfterEffects to edit all the assets to appear on beat, add 3D text, and keyframe using various transform functions.
