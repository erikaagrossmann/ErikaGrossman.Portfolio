Erika Grossman Logo

Created Fall 2025

For this project we were asked to use AfterEffects to create a Logo for anything we wanted. The only requirements were that it had to be over 5 seconds, and that there needed to be at least one sound in the video. I utilized the 3D text effect, keyframing, trim paths, and orientation/rotation to create a short and sweet logo for myself.
