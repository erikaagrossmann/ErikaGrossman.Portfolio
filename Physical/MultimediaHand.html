Multimedia Hand

Created Fall 2023

This piece was apart of a 12-week constant output collection of work. The theme of the collection could be anything of the students' choosing, and I chose to focus on the wrinkles and curves of the human hand.
For this week of the collection, I was paired with two other students who had different themes for their collections of work. All together we created a piece involving cardboard cut with a laser cutter as a frame, a heart made with colored pencils and markers, a hand grabbing down with graphite pencil, and the entire piece wrapped in red string.
