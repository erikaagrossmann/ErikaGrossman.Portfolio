Paper Quill Artwork

Created Spring 2022

This piece of artwork is made with a unique technique called paper quilling. Each coil or design of paper was hand rolled/folded into a desired shape and the glued onto the board. The brown and white paper in this piece is supposed to resemble the branches of the Tree of Life with a slight twist of the theme of love- shown as some of the branches are in the shape of a heart. I made this piece as a frame for my sister's Ketuvah- a marriage license in the Jewish religion.
